# Homelessness

The Observatory's next research domain. Structure is scaffolded; research
has not started.

Navigational notes for when it does:

- The illegal dumping investigation already produced encampment-adjacent
  findings — F6 (encampment reports are report-only, feeding scheduled
  CARE/CARE+ deployments, never individual work orders) and F20 (the
  citywide #1 dumping site went silent when the adjacent encampment was
  resolved) in [docs/observatory_findings.md](../docs/observatory_findings.md).
- The MyLA311 dataset (`../shared/datasets/`, documented in
  `../shared/references/`) includes ~53K `Homeless Encampment` cases and is
  already downloaded and modeled.
